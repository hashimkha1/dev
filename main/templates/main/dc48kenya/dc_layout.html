The promulgation of the Constitution of Kenya, 2010, introduced the devolved system of Government which resulted in the formation of the 47 Counties across the Country. The new constitution has greatly benefitted the Kenyans in general. However, this is not the same case with a group of Kenyans living in diaspora. It is on this ground that more than 4 million Diaspora Community resolved to pursue their constitutional right as Kenyan citizens.
