    Greetings, esteemed members of Diaspora County 48 Kenya. It is with great pride and joy, that I extend to you a warm welcome to our organisation. As your Global Chairperson (Governor), I am honoured to lead this incredible community of Kenyans in the diaspora. Together, we have forged an unbreakable bond founded on our shared heritage, aspirations, and interests, creating a powerful network of individuals, organisations, and businesses dedicated to advancing a better diaspora and a better Kenya.

    At Diaspora County 48 Kenya, we are driven by a common vision and mission to serve, promote, and advocate for a conducive environment that fosters a prosperous diaspora, ' both socially and economically. Our relentless dedication and commitment to achieving these goals is fueled by the unwavering belief that, together, we can make an indelible impact on the lives of our members and the communities we serve.

    As we embark on this exciting journey, we remain guided by our core values of unity, integrity, accountability, and transparency. These values are not only the cornerstone of our organization but also fundamental in creating a strong and sustainable community. I extend to you an open invitation to join us on this exhilarating journey and contribute your invaluable ideas, skills, and resources towards realizing our shared aspirations. Together, we can build a future that surpasses our wildest dreams for ourselves and our beloved children.

    Thank you for being an integral part of our very own Diaspora County 48 Kenya.

    Our Mission

    Our mission is to serve, promote, and advocate for a conducive environment for a prosperous diaspora socially, economically, and politically, and extend such benefits to their country. In addition, we safeguard the diaspora's cordial relationship with their respective host communities for mutual benefit.

    More about us ***

    We are citizens of Kenyan descent living outside the country and in 2014, we identified ourselves as members of our envisioned Diaspora County 48 Kenya meanwhile we registered DC48 Kenya as a nonprofit organization headed by Global Chairperson presiding over country-wise Kenya Community Groups (independent individuals, Community-based organizations, Faith-based organizations or businesses organizations owned by Kenyans) within twenty-two chapters globally.
